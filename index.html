FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I rent a car? To rent a car, simply browse our car rental portal website, select the desired car, choose the rental dates, and proceed with the booking process.
What documents do I need to rent a car? You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card for payment, and a form of identification such as a passport or ID card.
Can I cancel or modify my reservation? Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation. Please refer to our cancellation and modification policy for more information.
Are there any age restrictions for renting a car? Yes, there are age restrictions for renting a car. The minimum age requirement may vary depending on the country and car rental company. Please check the terms and conditions for specific age requirements.
Is insurance included in the rental price? Insurance coverage may vary depending on the car rental company and the type of insurance selected. Please review the insurance options during the booking process or contact our customer support for more information.
